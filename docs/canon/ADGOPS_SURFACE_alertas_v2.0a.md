## 0. What this surface is

`alertas` is an alerts-oriented ADG OPS surface.

Today it should be read as:
- a current stubbed development surface
- a visible preparation area
- a bridge toward stronger future service capability

It is not yet a fully working alert system.

---

## 1. Why it exists

The surface exists because monitoring and alerting are strategically aligned with the suite.

It points toward:
- notification utility
- future alert delivery
- broader user-facing service capability
- stronger ecosystem responsiveness

This makes it more important than a throwaway support page, even before its logic is fully built.
