## 0. What this surface is

`estadisticas` is the configurable analytics surface of ADG OPS.

It is the place where the user should be able to:
- explore sector statistics
- change views by dimensions and controls
- inspect patterns through a more configurable analytical lens
- move beyond raw procurement browsing into analytical reading

It is not the same thing as `barometro`.

---

## 1. Why it exists

The surface exists to convert procurement domain information into:
- configurable statistical views
- filtered readings
- comparative or segmented analytical interpretation

Its role is broader than a single automatic narrative.
It is the more adjustable analytics environment of the suite.

---

## 2. What the user sees

Current reading suggests an analytics area with:
- a shell/shared layout
- filter rail or control region
- analytics content area
- configurable statistical outputs
- relationship to the `barometro` surface within the broader analytics space

The exact visualization system is still maturing.
The important point is that the surface is currently more than a placeholder and should be documented as real.

---

## 3. What the user can do

The user should currently be able to:
- switch or configure analytical views
- use filters and controls
- read sector statistics through different dimensions
- move between analytics-related modes or areas
- use the surface as a configurable reading layer

This is the broader configurable analytics environment of ADG OPS.

---

## 4. Current behavior

Current runtime reading:
- recent work improved the shell and surrounding behavior
- for the present version, this surface is more acceptable than the licitaciones lane

Important:
- this does **not** mean final
- this does **not** mean deeply mature
- it means current runtime is more coherent than some neighboring areas

## 7. Relationships to other surfaces

### Relationship to licitaciones
`estadisticas` should be read as an analytical layer connected to the broader procurement domain.

### Relationship to barometro
This is the most important relationship:
- `estadisticas` = configurable analytics
- `barometro` = period-based interpretive analysis

They may share UI territory, but they are not the same surface truth.

### Relationship to future data architecture
Future improvements in data intake and structured storage will likely strengthen `estadisticas` materially.

---

## 8. Current issues / fragility

Current cautions:
- the surface is still basic relative to long-term ambition
- it may still rely partly on evolving shell and data assumptions
- it should not be mistaken for a finished analytics system

This is a meaningful current surface, but not a final one.

---

## 9. Intended evolution

`estadisticas` is likely to evolve toward:
- richer configurability
- stronger statistical depth
- clearer explanatory structure
- better coupling to future data architecture
- broader analytical usefulness across the suite

It should grow as the configurable analytics backbone, distinct from the interpretive role of `barometro`.
